Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2026

Introduction

Nudge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address (if you create an account)
  • Task and Reminder Data: Tasks, reminders, due dates, times, notes, and any other content you create in the app
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history, subscription status (processed through Apple/Google)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, interactions with tasks
  • Performance Data: Crash logs, error reports, app performance metrics

Push Notifications

We collect notification preferences and send push notifications for task reminders when you enable this feature. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Nudge app functionality
  • Send you task reminders and notifications
  • Process subscription purchases and manage your premium access
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Communicate with you about app updates or changes
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription and payment processing
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, subscription status, device identifiers
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Expo (React Native Framework)

  • Purpose: App infrastructure and updates
  • Data Collected: Device information, crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

Apple App Store / Google Play Store

  • Purpose: App distribution and payment processing
  • Data Collected: Payment information, purchase history
  • Privacy Policy: Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ | Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage and Security

  • Your task and reminder data is stored locally on your device
  • Subscription information is stored securely by RevenueCat and Apple/Google
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • No data is sold to third parties

Data Retention

  • Task Data: Stored locally on your device until you delete the app or clear data
  • Subscription Data: Retained by RevenueCat and Apple/Google per their policies
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for up to 2 years or until you request deletion

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications and decline optional data collection
  • Data Portability: Export your task data from the app

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nudge.noti@gmail.com or delete your account through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Nudge is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Legal Basis for Processing:
